About Me

A talented, passionate and hardworking artist who has a long record of accomplishment of creating original pieces of artwork through a variety of mediums. I have a strong artistic background, with a history of excellent artisanship; my priority is always customer needs and satisfaction. I work hard to stay current with the latest artistic tools and technologies. I am currently completing the Interactive Media Design with Algonquin College and have my background Bachelor of Fine Art in 1998 at Islamic Azad University.
